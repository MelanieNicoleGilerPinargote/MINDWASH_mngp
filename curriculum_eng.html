Melanie Nicole Giler Pinargote,

Graduated in Architecture from the Politecnico di Torino with a thesis focused on parametric processes in urban design. During my academic journey, I developed and applied an evolutionary algorithm for generating meta-design scenarios, which I then applied to a case study in Turin, the former Thyssen area, earning a commendation as a distinguished thesis.

From a professional perspective, I have gained solid skills in the use of BIM tools, algorithmic design, and computational tools based on visual programming, such as VPL. I had the opportunity to be a scholarship holder for the VPL and BIM courses in the Master's program in Architecture Construction City, further strengthening my expertise in these fields.

Curious and innovation-oriented, with a strong inclination for teamwork and problem-solving; this attitude led me to combine design and programming skills to create my personal website, conceived as a dynamic platform to showcase my portfolio and resume. My goal is to merge technology and architecture to develop cutting-edge sustainable solutions capable of managing the uncertainties of contemporary cities and exploring their possible futures. I strongly believe that the synergy between architecture and technology can provide effective and sustainable responses to the challenges of urban design.
